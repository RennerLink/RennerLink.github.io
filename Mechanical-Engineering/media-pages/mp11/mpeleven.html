Scissor Lift Skateboard

Constructed in February 2023, this skateboard like Lego vehicle has a massive scissor lift on it. The lift is powered by two small power functions motors and four large linear actuators. The board has 4 wheel steering and I never got around to building a platform on top of the lift.
