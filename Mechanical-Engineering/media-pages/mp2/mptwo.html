Robot Arm V4

Over 3ft tall when extended. This robotic arm was on four wheels to alow for an extra range of motion. The main challenge for this build was having structural stability while keeping the frame relatively thin and compact. All major joints used worm gears to lock them in place but allow for roation when a knob was turned.
